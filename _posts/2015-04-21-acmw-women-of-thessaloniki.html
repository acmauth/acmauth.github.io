ACM Student Chapter AUTH together with ACM-W CITY are celebrating the foundation of the ACM-W AUTH Chapter! 

11th May 2015 17:00 - 20:30

KE.D.E.A. (Red Building), Auditorium II, 

3is Septemvriou Str., Aristotle University of Thessaloniki

ACM-W Chapters across the globe, aim to raise awareness, encourage and inspire women to take part and excel in computer science!

In this event you will have the oppurtunity to learn and get inspired from distinguished invited speakers about Web Development, responsive design and new practices, Unity 3D Game Development, team work and more.

The working language of the event is English.
