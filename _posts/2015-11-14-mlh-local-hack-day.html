We are excited to announce our Local Hack Day hosting, on the 10th of October, from 9am to 9pm.

Local Hack Day

Local Hack Day is a 12 hour mini-hackathon on university campuses around the world that brings together the local hacker community to celebrate building awesome technology. Simultaneously other students around the world will be hosting their own Local Hack Days, resulting in the largest single day of student hacking ever. Throughout the day there will be awesome livestreams, chatrooms, bootstrapping presentations and fun activities that you can use to meet students in your local community and across the globe.

Venue information

Computer Science Department,

Aristotle University of Thessaloniki

Important stuff

  • Bring Your Own Device (aka BYOD). We will be providing the basics, i.e. internet connection and power supply.
  • Food and drinks will be available throughout the day.
  • Remember to seize the day and create something awesome.
