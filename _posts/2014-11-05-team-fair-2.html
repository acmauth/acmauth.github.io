This year our chapter participated at the TeamFair 2.0, 5-6 of November, co-organised by the univeristy-wide team, CoMvoS. During these two days our chapter interacted with other teams of university. Many students attended and got informed of our activities and projects, while there was also a presentation of the Unity 3D platform and our Unity project, Dragonstone Isle.
